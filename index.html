Why we exist

Big tech built great software.
They just made you pay with your freedom.

🏠

Self-Hosted First

Every product we build runs on your hardware, in your home, on your terms. No subscriptions. No lock-in. No one to answer to but yourself.

🔓

Fully Open Source

Everything we write is public. Read the code, audit it, fork it, contribute to it. If you can't inspect it, you can't trust it.

🔌

API First

Every tool ships with a complete public API. Automate it, integrate it, build on top of it. Your data should work for you — not the other way around.

📦

Easy to Install

Self-hosting shouldn't require a PhD. We obsess over installer quality and documentation so anyone can be up and running in minutes.
